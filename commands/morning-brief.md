Call focor_morning_brief to get today's cross-tool synthesis.

Present the briefing in this order:
1. **Calendar overview** — meetings today, any conflicts
2. **Overnight activity** — what happened in Slack/Email while they were away
3. **Overdue commitments** — open loops past due date
4. **Focus recommendation** — suggest what to tackle first

Keep it conversational. Don't dump raw data. Synthesize.
End with: "What do you want to dive into first?"
